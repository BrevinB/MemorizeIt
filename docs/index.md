## Features

- **Three Practice Modes** - Full Text, Hidden Words, and Blank Canvas
- **Smart Scheduling** - Spaced repetition reminds you when to review
- **Multiple Translations** - KJV, NIV, ESV, NLT, and more
- **Progress Tracking** - Watch your mastery grow over time
- **Daily Streaks** - Build consistency with streak tracking
